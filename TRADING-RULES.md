## üìÖ DAILY RULES (NON-NEGOTIABLE)

### Day Trading:
1. **Max 5-10 trades per day**
   - Jangan overtrade
   - Quality > quantity
   
2. **Daily profit target: 2-5%**
   - Realistic target
   - Hit target = STOP (jangan serakah)
   
3. **Daily loss limit: 3-5%**
   - Hit loss limit = STOP TRADING
   - No exception!
   - Come back tomorrow
   
4. **If loss 3x in a row ‚Üí STOP for 1 hour**
   - Take a break
   - Clear your mind
   - Review what went wrong
   
5. **If hit daily target ‚Üí STOP**
   - Jangan serakah
   - Profit secured
   - Come back tomorrow

### Scalping:
1. **Max 10-15 trades per day**
   - High frequency trading
   - Quick in, quick out
   
2. **Daily profit target: 3-7%**
   - From many small trades
   - 1% √ó 10 trades = 10%
   
3. **Daily loss limit: 2-3%**
   - Tighter limit (more trades)
   - Hit limit = STOP immediately
   
4. **If loss 3x in a row ‚Üí STOP for 30 minutes**
   - Faster break (scalping pace)
   - Reset mental state
   
5. **Max loss per trade: 0.3-0.5%**
   - Very tight stop loss
   - Cut loss instantly

## üìä POSITION SIZING RULES

### Day Trading:
- **Max 20-30% capital per trade**
- **Max 3-5 positions simultaneously**
- **Always keep 30-50% cash reserve**
- **Scale in/out (not all-in/all-out)**

### Scalping:
- **Max 15-20% capital per trade**
- **Max 3-5 positions simultaneously**
- **Always keep 50% cash reserve**
- **Quick in, quick out**

### Risk Calculation:
```
Position Size = (Account Size √ó Risk %) / (Entry Price - Stop Loss Price)

Example:
Account: Rp 100,000,000
Risk: 2% = Rp 2,000,000
Entry: Rp 1,000
Stop Loss: Rp 980
Difference: Rp 20

Position Size = 2,000,000 / 20 = 100,000 shares = 100 lot
```
