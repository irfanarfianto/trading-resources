## SCALPING SETUPS

```

- Volume meningkat
- Bid size > Ask size

Entry:

- Konfirmasi: Volume spike

Target: +1-2% (20-50 poin)
Stop Loss: -0.5% (10-15 poin)
Hold Time: 5-15 minutes
```

### Setup 2: Pullback Scalping
```
Kondisi:
- Uptrend kuat
- Pullback ke support/EMA
- Volume menurun saat pullback

Entry:
- Buy saat bounce dari support
- Konfirmasi: Bullish candle + volume

Target: +1-1.5% (15-30 poin)
Stop Loss: -0.3% (5-10 poin)
Hold Time: 3-10 minutes
```

### Setup 3: Range Scalping
```
Kondisi:
- Sideways di range jelas
- Support & resistance tested multiple times
- Low volatility

Entry:
- Buy di support, Sell di resistance
- Konfirmasi: Rejection candle

Target: +0.5-1% (range height)
Stop Loss: -0.3% (below support)
Hold Time: 2-5 minutes
```

### Setup 4: Opening Range Breakout
```
Kondisi:
- First 15 minutes (09:00-09:15)
- Identify high & low
- Wait for breakout

Entry:
- Buy breakout high OR Sell breakdown low
- Konfirmasi: Volume > average

Target: +2-3% (momentum)
Stop Loss: -0.5% (back to range)
Hold Time: 10-30 minutes
```
